# Cave Game

A first-person maze game built in Unity where you navigate a dark cave with only a flashlight to guide you. Touch a wall and you die.

## Game Rules

- Navigate through the maze and reach the **exit** to escape
- Do not touch the walls - any contact instantly kills you
- The cave is pitch black - you only have your flashlight
- On death, the YOU DIED screen appears - press R to respawn at the start

## Controls

- Move using **WASD**
- Use the **Mouse** to look around
- Sprint (2x speed) by holding **left shift**
- Click **R** to respawn after death
